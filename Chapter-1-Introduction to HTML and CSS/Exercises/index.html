Chicken Lasagna

"Find the best Chicken Lasagna Recipe; a comforting variation of the famous Italian meal with lots of cheese and cream.' Layered with tender shredded chicken, rich white sauce, gooey mozzarella, and perfectly cooked lasagna sheets, this easy-to-follow recipe is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or meal prepping. Quick tips, step-by-step photos, and variations included!"

Ingredients:
  • -Oil 2 tbs
  • -Onion Chopped 2
  • -Garlic Chopped 1 tbs
  • -Chicken Mince ½ kg
  • -Tomato Chopped 2
  • -Tomato Ketchup ½ cup
  • -Salt 1 tsp
  • -Red chili Crushed 1 tsp
  • -Black pepper Crushed 1 tsp
  • -Dried Oregano 1 tsp
  • -Tomato Paste 2 tbs
  • -Chicken stock or water ½ cup
  • -Capsicum Chopped 1 cup
  • White Sauce:
  • -Butter 2 tbs
  • -All-purpose flour 2 tbs
  • -Milk 2 & ½ cup
  • -Salt ½ tsp or to taste
  • -White pepper powder 1 tbs

Assembling
  1. White Sauce
  2. Lasagna Sheets 12 boiled as per pack's instructions
  3. Chicken filling
  4. White Sauce
  5. Cheddar Cheese 1 cup or as required
  6. Mozzarella Cheese 1 cup or as required
  7. Dried Oregano to sprinkle

Direction

For Chicken Filling:

  1. In pan, add oil and chopped onion and garlic, saute for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add chicken mince and saute until mince changes color
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato ketchup and mix well.
  4. Add salt, crushed red chilli, black pepper and chicken stock or water, cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add capscium, mix and set aside.

For White Sauce:

  1. In pan, add butter and let it melt, add all-purpose flour and mix well.
  2. Gradually add milk and keep stirring and mix continuously.
  3. Add salt and white pepper powder and cook until sauce thickenes.

Bake Time
  1. Grease baking dish with oil or butter, spread white sauce, cover it with lasagna sheets, add chicken filling then again pour white sauce, sprinkle cheddar cheese and mozzarella cheese.
  2. Repeat the same process.
  3. Sprinkle cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese and dried oregano.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 180° C or until cheese is golden brown.

Chicken Lasagna Is Ready!
